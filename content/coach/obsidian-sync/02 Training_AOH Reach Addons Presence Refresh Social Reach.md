# AOH Reach Add-ons

Morning Brief is not the main product. Use it as included visibility/reporting.

The stronger marketable add-ons are:

1. **Presence Refresh** - one-time setup before Reach.
2. **Social Reach** - guarded recurring pilot after the process is proven.

## Presence Refresh

Promise:

> Before we drive new attention to your business, we make sure you do not look inactive when prospects check you out.

Best for:

- stale social pages
- no recent blog/content
- good business with weak online proof
- clients about to start Cold Email Reach or Social Reach

Starter package:

- short online presence audit
- five catch-up social posts
- one useful blog post
- light cleanup recommendations
- proof links/screenshots
- next Reach angle

Suggested price:

- $399-$750 starter
- $950-$1,500 stronger package

## Social Reach

Promise:

> We find the conversations your future customers are already having and help your business show up usefully before competitors do.

This is not bots posting in groups. V1 is listening, scoring, drafting, and human-approved posting.

Best for:

- B2B and local services where trust matters
- owners with expertise but no time to watch social conversations
- businesses already selling by referrals/relationships
- clients who can approve comments quickly

Pilot rules:

- build 10 approved sources
- find 25 conversation opportunities
- draft 10 helpful comments
- human-post only the best 3-5
- track replies, DMs, profile views, booked calls, and buyer language

Suggested price after pilot:

- $500-$750/month light watch
- $950-$1,500/month active Social Reach
- $1,500+/month custom social selling team

## Guardrails

- No auto-commenting.
- No auto-liking.
- No fake accounts.
- No mass scraping.
- No posting where business participation is banned.
- Human approval before posting during v1.
- Answer first, pitch only when it naturally fits.
